The renter accepts sole and absolute responsibility for any damage, loss, theft, vandalism, or mechanical failure of the vehicle during the rental period. In the event of an accident or damage, the renter is liable for all repair expenses, downtime costs, and third-party liabilities in full.

2. Mandatory Pre-trip Walkaround Video:
For your safety and protection against false claims, you MUST record a continuous walk-around video of the vehicle's exterior and interior before leaving the pickup location. Make sure to capture all existing scratches, dents, and tire conditions. This video serves as the official condition record.

3. Collection & Return Policy:
The vehicle will be collected from your designated location at the end of the rental period. It must be made available for collection BEFORE the rental end date and time expires.

If the vehicle is not returned on time, late return charges of ₹1,000 per day will be levied in addition to the standard daily pro-rated rent. Repeated delays may result in legal action.

5. Traffic Violations & Tolls:
The renter is responsible for all traffic fines, parking tickets, speeding violations, and toll charges accrued during the rental period. Providers reserve the right to bill these to your profile later.

6. Fuel Policy:
The vehicle must be returned with the same fuel level as provided at pickup. Refueling charges plus actual fuel difference will be charged if returned with less fuel.
